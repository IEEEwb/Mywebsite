teacher student management

It was a hard time for all of us. We all faced problems during the corona pandemic. The main problem faced was the one that concerned shaping our little todders for our great future.

Problems faced

  • Good but old teachers not knowing the technologies.
    There are many teacher that have more than 30 year experience in teaching but fail to give their best due to lack of knowledge in technologies.
  • NO proper excess to internet or broadwane.

  • Less willing students to study
    there are many children who less wiling to study but will still join the session to disturb others who actually want to study

Solutions

participations

Students should be prompted by the teacher to take part in class. they should be asked questions based on what they learnt.

Task giving

Children should be given various task during the lecture. Task should all be out the study topics and should excite children and keep them active

wise class division

Students should be divided wisely based on their interest in studies and in subjects to get maximum productivity out of them.

A teacher working

How can teachers problem be solved?

Teacher should be given classes on how the particular online studying platform functions. He/she should be guided about all the features of that base to make the studies as unique and interesting as possible. Old teachers should be helped out of their problems so tha they can teach properly.

Parents role

How can parents play their role?                                 

Parents can play their role by constantly checking whether their child is studying or not. They can have rounds in the place where their child is sitting for lectures. They can ask them about what they did in lecture on that day.

Can there be a parents teacher interactions?                                     

Yes. there can be a parent teacher interaction via internet. Parents and teacher and regularly arrange meetings and talk about the productivity about the child.

If the above things along with some other rules by the individual institutes are followed then internet learning can really be converted into fun education.
